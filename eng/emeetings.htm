We meet on Saturday mornings at 10:30 for the Lord’s supper and a sermon. Once a month, at the first weekend, there is no meeting on Saturday morning and instead there is a meeting on Friday evening at 18:30. On Wednesdays at 19:00 we have a prayer meeting or a Bible study

All the meetings are held in the assembly hall at Yefet street number 42 in Jaffa. Those who come by bus can take bus number forty six from the central bus station in Tel-Aviv
